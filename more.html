TU VEUX
EN PARLER ?

In 2020, I created a question card game called Tu veux en parler ? ("Do you want to talk about it?"). With 120 questions split in 3 categories, this tool guides players towards kind, nuanced and healing conversations around mental health.

Early iteration of the game - January 2021

Inspired by my desire to normalize awkward conversations and my belief that opening up to others is key to break out of the shame and stigma, I started asking questions. This quickly turned into a full-on user research,with a total of 150+ people surveyed, 23 interviewed and countless exchanges on social media. I learned a lot. We want to talk about mental health, but need a trigger; it's difficult to be the first one to be vulnerable. We often lack the proper words to express how we feel, and especially to confort someone who's struggling. We feel a lot of shame, and can get stuck in the vicious circle of stigmatisation, assuming something is wrong with us and going further into isolation rather than seeking help. I also found out that the 18-25yo are the most interested in breaking the stigma. Having to face many economical, health and climate-related anxieties while going through important life transitions, young people were turning to TikTok and Instagram to share their struggles and find support, launching a generational conversation. Lastly, there was a demand for non-digital tools and for french-speaking initiatives. I joined the startlab incubator and together with mentor and fellow entreprenors I started creating the game When things eased of, I tested. I took cards i wrote questions on them. I must have iterate through hundreds of questions. I experienced and refined the rules. Organized games, set up an online presence (instagram and tiktok), it was all new. questions on cards, bi*in around brussels to drop them (still in loc*down)
