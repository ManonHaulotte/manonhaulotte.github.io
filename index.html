BelfiusWeb 2.0: Designing a Corporate and Public Banking Dashboard that works for everyone.

UX STRATEGY | INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE | CONCEPT DESIGN | CO-CREATION & WORKSHOP FACILITATION

BelfiusWeb, the digital solution for corporate and public banking, was facing issues with its redesign. I led a series of co-creation workshops with key stakeholders to align the team, and conducted a thorough usability audit and user research to identify opportunities for improvement. Through this process, I ended up redesigning the dashboard, facilitating the work of a wide variety of user profiles."

How might we facilitate greater public engagement with academic research?

UX Research | UI/UX Design | USER TESTING | VISUAL IDENTITY | Front-End

Created during the immersive UX Design program at Brainstation Toronto, biblio is an iOS app that aims to increase the reach and impact of academic research by making the findings easy to discover, comprehend and engage with outside of universities. This project gave me the opportunity to explore each step of the design process, from identifying and understanding the barriers to engagement, to finding and testing possible solutions to overcome them.

How to optimize the experience of getting a car insurance quote online?

UX Research | UX/UI DESIGN | USER TESTING

One of Capco's client, a major Canadian insurance company, wanted to fully rethink their online quote experience. It had to be fast, intuitive and appealing to younger customers, to build trust and to be consistent with the brand and existing patterns. Together with a cross-functional agile team, we researched, designed, tested and developed the flow for a new client getting a car insurance quote in Quebec, from entering their information to selecting a plan.
