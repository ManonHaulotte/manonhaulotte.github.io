Bonjour!

I am Manon, a psychology-trained, technology-obsessed designer and strategist. With over 6 years of experience in both startups and corporate environments, my skills cover the entire product design process.

As a creative problem solver and big picture thinker, I excel in ambiguous and uncharted design territory. I use research, pattern detection, system thinking, prototyping, and testing to make sense of complexity. I'm always asking "what if" and experimenting with new concepts to improve both the user experience and design processes. I also have a knack for maintaining visual consistency and have initiated multiple design systems.

Having worked either in consulting positions or as the first UXer on a team, I have experience leading design efforts and processes, on large scope, ambiguous projects and in close collaboration with developers, project managers, and stakeholders, spreading design culture in a company I'm passionate about design education, co-creation, and workshop facilitation. I currently teach design thinking methods and mentor junior designers.

My goal is to move towards managing design teams and processes, creating an environment where designers can thrive, improving communication between design and other teams, and increasing the visibility of design efforts and UX maturity in an organization. I would be thrilled by the opportunity to take on this type of role.

I'm also constantly exploring the evolution of human-computer interactions and currently delving deeper into AI and AI safety.
