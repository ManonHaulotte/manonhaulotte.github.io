Bonjour!
I'm Manon, a digital product designer*.

Naturally inquisitive, I've always had a keen interest in human behaviours and emotions: how do people perceive their world? What makes them tick? What motivates their decisions? I think that's why I've moved around a lot, why I'm never without a book and why I aim to design well-researched, intentional products: for the chance to experience, imagine or research different ways of thinking.

* I used to call myself a UX/UI designer. While that's still a big part of my job, "Product Designer" better reflects what I actually do. I keep an eye on the broader, holistic view and take into consideration not only the users' needs but also the business' goals and values. I enjoy being involved throughout the entire product development process: from identifying and investigating the problem to designing, articulating, testing, and shipping the solution (and beyond). Beside design, strategy and research, I dabble in front-end development and animation and strive to constantly expand my toolkit. I thrive in cross-functional teams. To put it simply, I'm a proud generalist.
